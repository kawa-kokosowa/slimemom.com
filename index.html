Hen Coop Software

Totally member-run worker coop for experiencers of transmisogyny

Who are we?

Anarchist communist consensus-oriented decision-making worker cooperative for those who experience transmisogyny.

  • All members of the cooperative are workers
  • There is no authority
  • All decisions are made by member consensus

For more details, including membership qualifications, consensus and member meeting process, please see the Hen Coop Handbook.

"For those who experience transmisogyny?"

Trans women and nonbinary people, at all kinds of intersections, e.g., race, sexuality (bisexual, lesbian, asexual, etc.), impoverishment, sex workers, mentally ill, chronically ill.

We allow anyone, including cis people to apply for membership, if it's by the referral of a member in our organization who experiences transmisogyny.

What do we do for members?

  • We facilitate a safe space for collaboration and learning, enforced by our code of conduct.
  • Evenly distributed shares of revenue made as an entity, e.g., donations, sponsorships, job ads to our Slack
  • Volunteer and learning experience
  • Contract opportunities

Can anyone join?

You can email lillian.gardenia.seabreeze@gmail.com. Memberships require an interview followed by member consensus. Membership is something we do not take lightly.

You will be expected to provide real value and contributions to the cooperative.

What kind of stuff do you do?

Largely, facilitate an encouraging space for members to work on and collaborate projects they own.

Facilitate an encouraging space for member-owned work. A group of members could start a project of their own inside our space, and the rights to that project are entirely theirs.
